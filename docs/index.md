# Welcome to Paris Survival Guide

![Paris Survival Guide](assets/img/hero_illustration.png)

Welcome to your essential companion for navigating Paris like a local. 

## 🎯 Our Mission

<div class="wotd-root-class" style="max-width: 600px;"></div>

Our mission is to equip English-speaking travelers with the core French vocabulary, cultural context, and confidence needed to thrive in Paris—from ordering a café to bargaining at the flea market.

## 👥 Who Is This For?
This guide is designed for English speakers who are planning a trip to Paris and want to go beyond the absolute basics. Whether you have zero exposure or a little bit of high school French, this app focuses on practical, real-world scenarios.
