# 3. Navigation, Transport & Numbers

Getting around Paris requires mastering the complex public transit system (RATP) and knowing basic numbers. The Metro is the lifeblood of the city.

## 1. The Numbers (1-10)
You need basic numbers for tickets, platforms, and times.

| Number | French | Pronunciation Guide |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **un / une** | ⟨uhn / oon⟩ |
| 2 | **deux** | ⟨duh⟩ |
| 3 | **trois** | ⟨trwah⟩ |
| 4 | **quatre** | ⟨kahtr⟩ |
| 5 | **cinq** | ⟨sank⟩ |
| 6 | **six** | ⟨sees⟩ |
| 7 | **sept** | ⟨set⟩ |
| 8 | **huit** | ⟨weet⟩ |
| 9 | **neuf** | ⟨nuhf⟩ |
| 10 | **dix** | ⟨dees⟩ |
| 20 | **vingt** | ⟨van⟩ |

## 2. Navigating the Metro and Buses
Paris transport uses the "Navigo" system (passes) or individual tickets.

* **Un ticket, s'il vous plaît.** ⟨uhn tee-kay, seel voo play⟩ — One ticket, please.
* **Un carnet de dix tickets.** ⟨uhn kar-nay duh dees tee-kay⟩ — A book of 10 tickets (often cheaper).
* **Où est la station de métro le/la plus proche?** ⟨oo ay lah stah-syon duh may-tro luh/lah ploo prosh⟩ — Where is the closest metro station?
* **C'est quelle ligne?** ⟨say kel leenyuh⟩ — Which line is it?
* **Je descends au prochain arrêt.** ⟨zhuh day-sahn oh proh-shan ah-ray⟩ — I am getting off at the next stop (useful for crowded buses).

## 3. Asking for Directions
When you inevitably get lost in the winding streets of the Marais or Montmartre, use these phrases. Always start with *Bonjour, excusez-moi de vous déranger...* (Hello, excuse me for bothering you).

| English Phrase | French Phrase | Pronunciation Guide |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Where is the Eiffel Tower? | **Où se trouve la tour Eiffel ?** | ⟨oo suh troov lah toor eh-fel⟩ |
| I am looking for the Louvre. | **Je cherche le musée du Louvre.** | ⟨zhuh shersh luh moo-zay doo loovr⟩ |
| Is it far? | **C'est loin ?** | ⟨say lwan⟩ |
| Turn left. | **Tournez à gauche.** | ⟨toor-nay ah gohsh⟩ |
| Turn right. | **Tournez à droite.** | ⟨toor-nay ah drwaht⟩ |
| Go straight ahead. | **Allez tout droit.** | ⟨ah-lay too drwah⟩ |

## 4. Airport Logistics (CDG / Orly)
* **Je vais à l'aéroport Charles de Gaulle.** ⟨zhuh vay ah lah-eh-roh-pohr sharl duh gohl⟩ — I am going to Charles de Gaulle airport.
* **Combien coûte la course vers...?** ⟨kohm-byan koot lah koors ver⟩ — How much is the fare to...? (Crucial for taxis).
