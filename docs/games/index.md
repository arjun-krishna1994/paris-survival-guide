### Why play these games?

Reading about French politeness rules (*Le vouvoiement*) is easy. Remembering to use *Vous* instead of *Tu* while a waiter stares at you impatiently is hard. 

These games use **active recall** and **stress mechanics** (like the falling timer in Suffix Station) to force your brain to retrieve vocabulary rapidly. This bridges the gap between studying a language and actually speaking it on the streets of Paris.
