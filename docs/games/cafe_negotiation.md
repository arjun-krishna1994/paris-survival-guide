# Le Café Parisien: Survival Simulator

Practicing phrases in a list is easy. Using them under the intense gaze of a busy Parisian waiter is an entirely different skill. 

Choose your waiter's personality and test your conversational agility. Remember the golden rule: **always establish politeness first.**
