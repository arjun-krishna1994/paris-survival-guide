# 4. Shopping & Daily Logistics

Beyond sightseeing, you'll need to buy groceries, perhaps some clothes, and manage your accommodation.

## 1. Grocery Shopping & The Market
Parisians shop frequently at local *boulangeries* (bakeries) and open-air markets.

* **Où se trouve le rayon...?** ⟨oo suh troov luh ray-ohn⟩ — Where is the ... aisle?
* **Donnez-moi une baguette, s'il vous plaît.** ⟨dohn-nay-mwah oon bah-get, seel voo play⟩ — Give me one baguette, please. (Perfectly polite in a bakery context).
* **C'est combien? / Combien ça coûte?** ⟨say kohm-byan / kohm-byan sah koot⟩ — How much is it?
* **Avez-vous des sacs réutilisables?** ⟨ah-vay voo day sahk ray-oo-tee-lee-zahbl⟩ — Do you have reusable bags?

## 2. Clothing & Shoe Shopping (Les Magasins)
Warning: French clothing and shoe sizes use a completely different system than the US or UK!

| English Request | French Phrase | Pronunciation Guide |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Where are the fitting rooms? | **Où sont les cabines d'essayage ?** | ⟨oo sohn lay kah-been day-say-ahzh⟩ |
| I am a size 38 | **Je fais du 38** | ⟨zhuh fay doo trahnt-weet⟩ |
| It's too big / small | **C'est trop grand / petit** | ⟨say tro grahn / puh-tee⟩ |
| I'm just looking, thank you. | **Je regarde seulement, merci.** | ⟨zhuh ruh-gahrd suhl-mahn, mair-see⟩ |

## 3. Bargaining at Flea Markets (Les Puces)
Paris has famous flea markets like Les Puces de Saint-Ouen. Bargaining is expected, but keep it polite and respectful.

* **C'est un peu cher, non ?** ⟨say tuhn puh sher, nohn⟩ — It's a bit expensive, no?
* **Vous pouvez me faire un meilleur prix ?** ⟨voo poo-vay muh fer uhn may-yur pree⟩ — Can you give me a better price?
* **D'accord, je le prends.** ⟨dah-kohr, zhuh luh prahn⟩ — Alright, I'll take it.

## 4. Hotel Check-in & Check-out
* **J'ai une réservation au nom de...** ⟨zhay oon ray-zair-vah-syon oh nohm duh⟩ — I have a reservation under the name...
* **Est-ce que le petit-déjeuner est compris ?** ⟨es-kuh luh puh-tee-day-zhuh-nay ay kohm-pree⟩ — Is breakfast included?
* **À quelle heure dois-je libérer la chambre ?** ⟨ah kel ur dwah-zhuh lee-bay-ray lah shahmbr⟩ — What time is checkout?
* **Je voudrais régler ma note.** ⟨zhuh voo-dray ray-glay mah noht⟩ — I would like to settle my bill.
