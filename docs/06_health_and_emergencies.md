# 6. Survival, Health & Emergencies

While Paris is generally very safe, you need to know how to ask for help, navigate a pharmacy, or deal with emergencies. The French emergency numbers are 15 (Ambulance), 17 (Police), and 18 (Fire). The pan-European emergency number is 112.

## 1. Asking for Help
* **Aidez-moi ! / Au secours !** ⟨ay-day-mwah / oh suh-koor⟩ — Help me! / Help!
* **J'ai besoin d'aide, s'il vous plaît.** ⟨zhay buh-zwan ded, seel voo play⟩ — I need help, please.
* **Pouvez-vous m'aider ?** ⟨poo-vay-voo may-day⟩ — Can you help me?
* **Je suis perdu(e).** ⟨zhuh swee pair-doo⟩ — I am lost.

## 2. At the Pharmacy (La Pharmacie)
French pharmacies (marked by a glowing green cross) are the first stop for minor ailments. Pharmacists are highly trained and can dispense advice and over-the-counter medication.

| Medical Issue | French Phrase | Pronunciation Guide |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| I am sick | **Je suis malade** | ⟨zhuh swee mah-lahd⟩ |
| I have a headache | **J'ai mal à la tête** | ⟨zhay mahl ah lah tet⟩ |
| I have a stomach ache | **J'ai mal au ventre** | ⟨zhay mahl oh vahntr⟩ |
| I have a fever | **J'ai de la fièvre** | ⟨zhay duh lah fyevr⟩ |
| I'm coughing a lot | **Je tousse beaucoup** | ⟨zhuh toos boh-koo⟩ |

* **Je voudrais quelque chose pour...** ⟨zhuh voo-dray kel-kuh shoze poor⟩ — I would like something for...
* **Avez-vous des pansements ?** ⟨ah-vay voo day pahns-mahn⟩ — Do you have bandaids/plasters?

## 3. Dealing with Police / Theft
Pickpocketing is common in tourist areas. If your belongings are stolen, you'll need to file a report (*porter plainte*) at a police station (*commissariat*).

* **On m'a volé mon sac / mon portefeuille.** ⟨ohn mah voh-lay mohn sahk / mohn pohrt-fuhy⟩ — Someone stole my bag / my wallet.
* **J'ai perdu mon passeport.** ⟨zhay pair-doo mohn pahs-pohr⟩ — I have lost my passport.
* **Où est le commissariat de police le plus proche?** ⟨oo ay luh koh-mee-sah-ryah duh poh-lees luh ploo prosh⟩ — Where is the nearest police station?
* **Je voudrais porter plainte.** ⟨zhuh voo-dray pohr-tay plant⟩ — I would like to file a police report.
